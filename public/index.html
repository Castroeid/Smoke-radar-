Brisket

Brisket comes from the lower chest and carries a lot of connective tissue. It becomes rich and tender when cooked slowly.

Profile

Fat: Medium Tenderness: Low raw / High when slow-cooked

Best Uses

  • Smoking
  • Long braise
  • Low and slow oven cook

Tip

Brisket rewards patience. Resting is almost as important as the cook itself.
